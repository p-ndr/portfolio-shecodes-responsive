About Me

I am a Graduate Computer Science student at University of Tehran, with research interests in Type Theory, Logic, Theory of Computer Science and Quantum Computation. I have received my BS in Computer Science from Sharif University of Technology, through which I gained a good sum of experience working with AI, Computer Vision and Data Science tools. I have served as intern in a number of Iranian Companies, including Behkhaan and Hesaba, gaining hands-on experience with Python, R, C#, SQL and various tools, and have been one of the participants of SheCodes Workshops, learning front-end development and design.
Besides my interest in tech, I'm fluent in English, and understand some other languages.
